Sara and Alan are trying to find their father's relics: magic objects that helped him to conquer their enemies and to create his empire. After succeed, Edward I (father of Sara and Alan), hid the relics in the empire's forest so no one could use them again. Now that Edward I has just died, Sara and Alan want to find the relics. After a whole day searching for them, it is time to back home. Once more, help Sara and Alan to be reunited so they can return home together.
